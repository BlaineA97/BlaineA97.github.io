Gaming - What is it?

~A Brief History~

In 1983 the original Nintendo Entertainment System was released to the American public and paved the way for the industry we regonize today.

They faced overwheamling opposition after the Great Gaming Depression caused by the lack of standards provided to devlopers during the time the Atari and its predecessors.

While there have been many incarnations of game consoles and games, they all retain the same simple goal: To engage and entertain.
